Jimmy Ruan

My name is Jimmy Ruan, and I am a Software Engineer in the University of Michigan. I have researched the linguistic change and the language policy as a result of Turkish immigration in the Netherlands. This is a topic that I find interesting because I have learned that, contrary to the language policy of the United States and other European nations, the Netherlands allowed Turkish immigrants to attend education in their own language and culture (although that has changed in recent years, as they are required to know Dutch to become a citizen).
